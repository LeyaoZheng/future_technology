The Introduction of AR / VR :

Augmented reality (AR) is an interactive experience that combines the real world and computer-generated 3D content, while Virtual reality (VR) is a simulated experience that employs 3D near-eye displays and pose tracking to give the user an immersive feel of a virtual world.
